Luxury Home #1 — Walkthrough Controls
Move: WASD or arrow keys. Hold Q with the arrows to turn and look, or use the mouse.
Modes: Walk follows floors and stairs. Press F for Fly; use Space/C for altitude and hold Shift to run.
Views: E opens FRONT, B opens BACK, J opens SIDE L, and L opens SIDE R. Each view frames the full property and acreage from an elevated angle. R toggles the roof, U toggles slabs, X resets, and the mouse wheel zooms.
Doors: Double-click a recognized entry, interior, or garage door to open or close it.

Height: Hold UP or DOWN for vertical movement. DOWN stops at the land and UP stops at the property viewing limit. The small center RESET button returns to the starting walk view.
